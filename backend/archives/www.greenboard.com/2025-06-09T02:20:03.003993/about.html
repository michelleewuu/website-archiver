About Greenboard

AI to increase human agency in support of a culture of compliance

Greenboard was founded by technologists who set out to build compliance software so good that not using Greenboard would become "tantamount to negligence in vendor selection."
Founded by Dave Feldman
and Ed Schembor
Headquarters in

Our Vision

Materially better compliance software

In 2023, we founded Greenboard after noticing first-hand how challenging it had become for financial institutions to keep up with ever-changing SEC regulation.

We went back to the drawing board on compliance software (hence the name Greenboard) and formulated our thesis. Financial firms needed an entirely new category of financial compliance software - a financial compliance operating system. We set out to build software that solves for the two problems that plague compliance programs most: engaging personnel to build a culture of compliance and maintaining reliable and actionable data to make quality compliance assessments.

Our Commitment

Serving compliance people

In a world where more and more can be automated we firmly believe that not everything should be. Our fundamental belief is that in areas that require a high degree of nuance and subjectivity - such as regulatory compliance - no matter how good computational systems become human input will continue to drive tremendous value. Instead of focusing on building "AI Agents" to replace personnel, we are building AI technology to expand each compliance person's efficiency and agency.
